ChES brings you an exciting opportunity on Cracking Tech interviews – A holistic Hands-on Workshop by Anand Venkataraman, a crash course before placements riving all the important topics.

The workshop will be conducted in two sessions and will be held on 28th November, 2021 (Sunday). It will be a live virtual session, through zoom meeting.

WORKSHOP OVERVIEW

Session 1 (9 AM – 12 PM):
Concepts and approach for cracking coding interviews.
In this session you will get to learn to apply a problem-solving technique for solving technical questions, in a simulated interview environment. We will take 2 to 3 key concepts in the domain of algorithms and Data structures, device real time interview level questions and solve the questions by applying the specific technique, which be a fool proof method to solve any technical question.

Even if an aspirant practices this technique for one day before the interview, there will be a significant impact for the aspirant, during the interview.
This session is your ultimate weapon for your preparation for coding interviews!!

Key Take away from the session:

  • A clear, fool proof technique to solve technical questions
  • A simulated interview environment, where an in-depth implementation of the technique on key questions related to Algorithms and Data structures
  • Tips and tricks for Interview Prep, DOs and DON'Ts, Soft skills tips and resources for further preparation

Session 2 (2 PM - 5 PM):
Data Science and Machine Learning with Python.
How about learning to predict stock prices real time, using Python? Sounds exciting? This is exactly what you will be doing in this session.

You will perform exploratory data analytics on real-time stock price data, by generating power visualizations like candlesticks and learn engaging storytelling techniques, as a data scientist. This is the most important skill for an entry level data scientist i.e., to be able to convert complex technical details into an interesting story and explain it to the manager. You will learn this here, in the workshop.

You will also learn predictive machine learning techniques (Time series analysis), which are generally used to predict stock prices.
You will do all of the above, hands-on, with Python, in live Kaggle kernels. This session is your ultimate weapon for your preparation for Data Science interviews!!

Key Take away from the session:

  • Effective storytelling techniques for Data Scientist with live examples.
  • In depth analytics of financial data and prediction algorithms, which are generally good to have in your CVs, (especially for Banks and consulting firms)
  • Building your Data Science portfolio, sneak-peek into other Machine Learning algorithms, trends in AI and resources for further preparation

We believe that these sessions will be helpful for you to gain essential knowledge in Data science & Machine learning using python..

ANAND VENKATRAMAN

Anand Venkataraman, an alumnus of IIT Madras, from Dept. of Chemical engineering, is a seasoned professional with 14 years of experience in the field of analytics and consulting across Finance and Engineering domains.
He holds an International MBA in Finance from United Business Institutes, Belgium. He has rich experience in the domain of Investment banking, product. control and quantitative finance.
Anand specializes in data science and has a strong understanding of mathematical fundamentals such as linear algebra, multivariable calculus, inferential statistics, neural networks, and algorithms, as well as practical data science using languages such as R/Python/MATLAB, tools such as SQL, MySQL, and MS Excel.

He has developed applications using text mining, natural language processing, and various other machine learning and deep techniques. He has won the silver medal in the Kaggle Global Forecast challenge in 2020. He is also an avid blogger and written interesting blogs machine learning.
