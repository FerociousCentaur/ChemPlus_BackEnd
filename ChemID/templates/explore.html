Greetings from ChES, IITM!
ChES (Chemical Engineering Society) stands as the face of Chemical Engineering Department at IIT Madras. We conduct several workshops, events to help students know about various opportunities available in chemical engineering. We do also conduct workshops on various other fields such as Data science, Machine learning etc and assist students pursue career in field of their interest.

This time we bring you an exciting opportunity to learn Coding concepts with python and Data science & machine learning with python by Anand Venkataraman, a crash course before placements riving all the important topic.

The workshop will be conducted in two sessions and will be held on 28th November.

WORKSHOP OVERVIEW

Session 1 (9 AM – 12 PM):
Concepts and approach for cracking coding interviews.
Key Highlights: In this session you will get to learn important concepts like Data structures, Algorithms which are important to crack coding interviews. Simulated environment will be formed to crack interview sessions. Also, a dedicated hour for secrets, tips and tricks for Interview Prep, resume building and presentation skills.

  • Timeline of the session - 3 hrs - 0.5 hr Introduction coding guidelines - (1+1) hr - Data Structures & algorithms - 0.5 hr resources Dos and Don'ts
  • Key takeaway: In-depth Problem-solving approach that will be asked in interviews template

Session 2 (2 PM - 5 PM):
Data Science and Machine Learning with Python.
Key Highlights: In this session you will get to learn important skills like visualization, analytics, data mining (with live practice on online kernels) and concepts linked to statistics and mathematics, which would be important to crack your Data Science interviews. Also, a dedicated hour for improving your profile and build your portfolio.

  • Timeline of the session - 3 hrs - 0.5 hr Introduction - 1 hr - Data Visualisation - 1 hr Machine Learning – Dos and Don’ts Interview focused
  • Key takeaway: Effective Data Interpretation and ML output

We believe that these sessions will be helpful for you to gain essential knowledge in Data science & Machine learning using python..

ANAND VENKATRAMAN

Anand Venkataraman, an alumnus of IIT Madras, from Dept. of Chemical engineering, is a seasoned professional with 14 years of experience in the field of analytics and consulting across Finance and Engineering domains. He holds an International MBA in Finance from United Business Institutes, Belgium. He has rich experience in the domain of Investment banking, product. control and quantitative finance. Anand specializes in data science and has a strong understanding of mathematical fundamentals such as linear algebra, multivariable calculus, inferential statistics, neural networks, and algorithms, as well as practical data science using languages such as R/Python/MATLAB, tools such as SQL, MySQL, and MS Excel. He has developed applications using text mining, natural language processing. deep learning, and machine learning techniques. He has won the silver medal in the Kaggle Global Forecast challenge in 2020.
